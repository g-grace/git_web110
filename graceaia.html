AIA Section

Audience Issues,Concerns,Needs Approach

Bikers

Age 25-50, male & female, sophisticated, trend-setting, passion for outdoor lifeestyles and activites, stay fit, love social networks, foodie, dreamer. A, B, B- socio-economic status; executives, manager, senior, mid-adults. He or she is a dreamer, adventurer, creative and idealistic. Importantly, he or she already has a prior knowledge about bikes.
  • Join his/her gang (bike society)
  • Have a great passion about having, buying, maintaining a bike
  • Look for a high quality bike
  • Care for a greater speed
  • Need a unique, classy and sustainable bike
  • Care for trust, good aftersales service and maintenance from a seller
  • Posses prior knowledge about bikes
  • Customers usually have product knowledge before coming to the store
  • Know what he/she wants
  • Promote the community and WOM Marketing
  • Link to Facebook Page
  • Go professional yet low-key friendly approaches
  • Liason with other bike societies, media, events
  • Easily browse website with product details and images available
  • Contact Us and responses to comments fast

Business Owners

Age 38-55, male & female, entrepreneur mindset, proud, networking, rich but less accessible to a bike or outdoor activities. He or she does not have any prior knowledge about a bike's functionality and features.He/she looks for a tangible and hand-on the decision on his/her own without intimidating or hard-sell.Take sometimes to decide to purchase a thing.
  • To find to to do an outdoor activity
  • Do not have a high passion about a bike
  • Do not have any prior knowledge about a bike
  • Looking around for a bike and seek information
  • High budget but low knowledge about a bike
  • Follow his/her social networks and need to be in a social status
  • Need assistance from a seller but relying on his/her own judgement
  • To be existed over the internet and tied up with events
  • Providing clear product details and easily to browse around
  • Have partners' links available as trusted sites
  • Providing knowledge and educate channel about bikes and functionality
  • Go soft-sell; They do not like approaching too hard
  • Direct marketing and promotions needed

Expatriats

Age 36-58, male & female, outgoing, active and stay fit. Residing temporary or newly relocated. Low knowledge about bike shops and less accible to networking society. Looking for an outdoor sports and individuality. He/she is confident and on-the-move. Internet savvy, inclining to reliable sources with a professional looks.

  • Want to stay fit
  • Shop for reliable sources and seller.
  • English is a must
  • Look for a clear product information, price, and method of payment
  • Need a clear location map and hours of operation
  • Look for something movable and flexible
  • High quality and consistant aftersales service is a must
  • Customer voices
  • To provide a clear, simple, yet professional store and product information
  • Provide contents in a grammartically English
  • Place partners' details and brand establising
  • Tell them "About Us", story of the shop
  • Have customer voice/ brand ambassadors available
  • State a clear contact information, map, operating hours and phone number
  • Events, news, promotions needed
